Our Team

The company

Our Team was formed in Silver Spring , Maryland by Alex Boti.  While working as a repair technician for a computer retail store. Alex Boti noticed that several businesses were frustrated with their computer needs. They couldn’t afford to have full time IT employees but needed the peace of mind of knowing that they computers problems would be quickly taken care of whenever they arose. Alex Boti came up with the idea of offering small businesses and home users computer expertise.
He then met with serveral friends and approached them with the idea. Together they setup the architecture of the business.
