The way we setup the price
As of Spring 2008, Techxel Computer Services updated its price services as follow:

Free Diagnostic (for any machine brought to our office)

For repair done in our office: Free diagnostic applies. The customer is then contacted and an estimate is given. Charges for the repair will depend on parts and softwares installed as well as labour.
